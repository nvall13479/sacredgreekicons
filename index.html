Authentic Hand-Painted Religious Art

Naturalistic Sacred Art

Combining classic religious themes with a naturalistic approach, this style emphasizes the grace and serenity of the sacred figures. Through diligent brushwork, soft shadows, and a captivating gaze, these works seek to establish a deep emotional connection and spiritual warmth with the viewer. Hand-painted with care on MDF or canvas and set in elegant frames, they serve as a timeless window to faith for any contemporary space.

Traditional Iconography

Our studio also creates works following the timeless paths of traditional iconography. Painted on thick, solid wood, these icons feature genuine 22K gold leaf, staying true to ancient spiritual forms.

Based in the historic port of Piraeus, Greece, our studio is strictly committed to the authentic craft of hand-painting. We never use mechanical means or digital prints.

Each piece is created with devotion using high-quality acrylics, oils and traditional egg tempera. Our mission is to offer these genuine pieces of Greek spiritual craftsmanship at highly affordable prices, making authentic hand-painted art accessible to every home.
